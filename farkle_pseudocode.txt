Game will start by asking how many players, that raw input will be stored in a variable for number of players. 

For each turn six dice will be initialted. Dice will be a class that has methods roll and keep. Player gets to decide which dice he wants to keep and which to roll again. 
Player will be asked if he/she would like to continue to roll or end their turn.

Upon ending their turn their total will be calculated and added to their score. 

Does user input their own total or does computer add it up for them?

First player to reach a score of 10,000 points wins.
